# Wiki Library Wishlist

A curated list of 100 books to build comprehensive domain wikis for the Excel AI Agent system.
Books are organized by domain (Finance, Excel, Accounting, Data Science, Markets) and marked with priority.

**Legend:**
- 🔴 **Critical** — Core reference, should be ingested first
- 🟠 **High** — Important for depth and specialization
- 🟡 **Medium** — Useful for edge cases and advanced topics
- 🟢 **Nice to have** — Expands breadth and cross-domain knowledge

---

## 🏦 FINANCE & VALUATION (40 books)

### Core Valuation

1. 🔴 **Valuation: Measuring and Managing the Value of Companies** — McKinsey & Company (Koller, Goedhart, Wessels)
   The bible of corporate valuation. Covers DCF, multiples, real options, and value creation frameworks.

2. 🔴 **Investment Valuation: Tools and Techniques for Determining the Value of Any Asset** — Aswath Damodaran
   Comprehensive valuation methodologies with practical Excel examples. The academic gold standard.

3. 🔴 **The Little Book of Valuation** — Aswath Damodaran
   Concise introduction to valuation principles. Perfect for wiki concept pages.

4. 🔴 **Applied Corporate Finance** — Aswath Damodaran
   Bridges theory and practice. Excellent for WACC, capital structure, and investment decisions.

5. 🔴 **Investment Banking: Valuation, LBOs, M&A, and IPOs** — Joshua Rosenbaum & Joshua Pearl
   The IB practitioner's handbook. Step-by-step models for DCF, LBO, M&A.

6. 🔴 **Financial Modeling and Valuation: A Practical Guide to Investment Banking and Private Equity** — Paul Pignataro
   Excel-based modeling from scratch. Perfect for formula reference wiki pages.

7. 🟠 **Valuation Workbook: Step-by-Step Exercises and Tests** — McKinsey & Company
   Practical exercises to test and reinforce valuation concepts.

8. 🟠 **Corporate Valuation for Portfolio Investment** — Robert F. Bruner
   Focus on valuation from an investor's perspective.

9. 🟠 **Valuing Companies in Bankruptcy and Distressed Situations** — Ian Ratner
   Specialized valuation for non-going-concern scenarios.

10. 🟡 **The Dark Side of Valuation** — Aswath Damodaran
   Valuing difficult companies: young firms, declining firms, financial firms.

### M&A and LBO

11. 🔴 **Mergers, Acquisitions, and Other Restructuring Activities** — Donald DePamphilis
   Comprehensive M&A guide covering strategy, valuation, and execution.

12. 🔴 **Leveraged Buyouts: A Practical Guide to Investment Banking and Private Equity** — Paul Pignataro
   LBO modeling from scratch with Excel templates.

13. 🟠 **The Art of M&A Strategy** — Alexandra Reed Lajoux
   Strategic thinking behind M&A transactions.

14. 🟠 **M&A: A Practical Guide to Doing the Deal** — Jeffrey C. Hooke
   Step-by-step deal execution guide.

15. 🟠 **Private Equity: History, Governance, and Operations** — Harry Cendrowski
   PE fund mechanics and investment process.

16. 🟡 **The Masters of Private Equity and Venture Capital** — Robert Finkel
   Case studies from top PE investors.

17. 🟡 **Barbarians at the Gate** — Bryan Burrough & John Helyar
   RJR Nabisco LBO case study. Historical context.

### Corporate Finance

18. 🔴 **Principles of Corporate Finance** — Richard Brealey, Stewart Myers, Franklin Allen
   The standard corporate finance textbook. Covers everything from NPV to options.

19. 🔴 **Corporate Finance** — Jonathan Berk & Peter DeMarzo
   Modern approach with strong emphasis on data and Excel.

20. 🟠 **Fundamentals of Corporate Finance** — Ross, Westerfield, Jordan
   Accessible introduction to corporate finance.

21. 🟠 **Capital Budgeting and Long-Term Financing Decisions** — Seitz & Ellison
   Deep dive into investment decision frameworks.

22. 🟡 **The Handbook of Corporate Finance** — Glen Arnold
   Practical guide for financial managers.

### Fixed Income & Derivatives

23. 🔴 **Fixed Income Securities: Tools for Today's Markets** — Bruce Tuckman & Angel Serrat
   Bond pricing, yield curves, and fixed income analytics.

24. 🟠 **The Handbook of Fixed Income Securities** — Frank Fabozzi
   Comprehensive reference for bond markets.

25. 🟠 **Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives** — John C. Hull
   The standard derivatives text. Black-Scholes, Greeks, hedging.

26. 🟡 **Interest Rate Risk Modeling** — Sanjay K. Nawalkha
   Advanced fixed income risk management.

27. 🟡 **Credit Risk: Pricing, Measurement, and Management** — Darrell Duffie
   Credit modeling and default risk.

### Risk & Portfolio Management

28. 🔴 **Modern Portfolio Theory and Investment Analysis** — Edwin Elton, Martin Gruber
   Mean-variance optimization, CAPM, APT.

29. 🟠 **Value at Risk: The New Benchmark for Managing Financial Risk** — Philippe Jorion
   VaR methodologies and risk measurement.

30. 🟠 **Financial Risk Management: A Practitioner's Guide** — Steve Allen
   Practical risk management for financial institutions.

31. 🟡 **The Handbook of Risk Management** — Philippe Carrel
   Comprehensive risk management frameworks.

### Real Estate & Alternative Investments

32. 🟠 **Real Estate Finance and Investments** — William Brueggeman & Jeffrey Fisher
   REIT valuation, real estate cash flows.

33. 🟡 **The Handbook of Alternative Investments** — Peter Temple
   Private equity, hedge funds, commodities.

34. 🟡 **Investing in REITs** — Ralph L. Block
   Real estate investment trusts analysis.

### Industry-Specific Valuation

35. 🟡 **Valuation of Technology Companies** — Marco Trombetta
   Valuing high-growth tech firms.

36. 🟡 **Bank Valuation and Value-Based Management** — Jean Dermine
   Special considerations for financial institutions.

37. 🟡 **Energy Valuation and Risk Management** — Glen Swindle
   Commodity-linked cash flows and valuation.

38. 🟡 **Healthcare Finance and Valuation** — Thomas R. Prince
   Hospital and healthcare company valuation.

39. 🟡 **The Valuation of Intangible Assets** — Margaret Blair
   Intellectual property and brand valuation.

40. 🟢 **Wine Economics: Quantitative Studies and Empirical Applications** — Eric Giraud-Héraud
   Niche asset valuation example.

## 📝 NOTES

- **Language**: English books are preferred for technical accuracy, but Italian translations are acceptable if they exist.
- **Editions**: Latest editions are preferred for accounting standards (GAAP/IFRS change frequently).
- **PDF Quality**: Searchable PDFs (not scanned images) are required for text extraction.
- **Copyright**: Ensure you have the right to use these books for personal knowledge base construction.
